The issue with 'just checking' social media is that it doesn't tend to end. You get stuck on it. Also it gets addictive, it just encourages more due to the power of intermittent reinforcement.

When you feel the urge to check just substitute with another action.

I should setup emails for any replies and notifications for post on the forum I moderate. But you do not need to just be there all the time.

It will consume you and it is never ending. But it will turn into a huge time sink as most of it is just quick takes and so on that fade. You can't really accumulate knowledge this way.

I wrongly thought that I needed to absorb all info. But simply no brain can absorb all the info, you can only absorb principles. 

According to ChatGPT, evidence indicates checking is associated with worse mental health outcomes more than total time spent. Best avoided.
