#human-written 
I just failed to understand that the internet was actually a problem. The basic issue is **there is no stopping point** unlike a textbook.

It is just more and more stuff grabbing your attention.

I used to think it was fine for "downtime" but it prevents your brain from getting the rest it needs in downtime. I had no idea this was happening ***until I got to 30!***

I didn't realise that I really need structured direction, ideas are fine but if you don't do anything with them, they are forgotten quickly. Also just ideas isn't enough, you need ***good ideas**.

ChatGPT has the same issue, there is no end, nothing ever consolidates.

The new approach is to have a "once a day" check for this stuff. Check Reddit for example once in the morning.
