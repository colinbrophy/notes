I think it is because they don't actually reflect actual ideas you had. AI just generates plausible ideas, it doesn't know if they are good or not.

Idea generation is not a bottleneck in thinking really, it's more about *good ideas* which only come about after learning and hard practice. Good ideas are battle tested against reality, simple AI generated ones are not.

AI does well in topic where you don't know much about it, it can of course write a better note on a topic you know nothing about. But that isn't that useful for you, the point of a [[Personal Knowledge Management]] system is as a memory aid for your own ideas. It helps tag and park them so you can revisit them later.
